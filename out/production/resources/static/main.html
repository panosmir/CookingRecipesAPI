CookingRecipesAPI Endpoints list

Recipes endpoint (/recipes)

  • /all (Shows all the recipes)
  • /all/{id} (Shows a recipe by id)
  • /all/userId/{id} (Shows the recipes that a user has created)
  • /all/userId/create (Create a recipe)
  • /all/findbyTitle/{title} (Find a recipe by title)
  • /delete (Deletes a recipe)
  • /update (Updates recipes data)
  • /ingredients/{recipe_id} (Shows the ingredients of a recipe)
  • /ingredients/add (Map ingredients to a recipe)
  • /ingredients/findByIngredients (Search a recipe by ingredients)

User endpoint (/users)

  • /create (Register a user)
  • /findUser (Login a user)

Categories endpoint (/categories)

  • /all (Shows all categories)
  • /findById/{id} (Shows a category by id)

Ingredients endpoint (/ingredient)

  • /all (Shows all the ingredients)
  • /findById/{id} (Shows ingredients by id)
  • /findByTitle/{title} (Find an ingredient by its title)
  • /findByCategoryId/{id} (Shows ingredients by category)
